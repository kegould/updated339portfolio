about

I am a passionate information science major, hoping to further develop my knowledge of information environments and data science.

I am a rising Senior at the University of Michigan-Ann Arbor from Dallas, Texas. I am studying in the school of Information, focusing on Information Analysis and additionally am pursuing a minor in food studies. Outside of class, I work as a Product Management Intern for Slytrunk, am involved in the University's FinTech club, and volunteer with my Sorority, Alpha Chi Omega. When I'm not partaking in any of these activities, I love to cook, workout, and go on long walks throughout Ann Arbor.

Some of my relevant coursework includes SI 330: Data Manipulation, SI 425: Introduction to User Modelling, SI 339: Web Design, Development and Acessibility, and SI 206: Data-Oriented Programming. I am proficient in Python, HTML, CSS, Javascript, SQL, Cypress, numpy, and Pandas. Additionally, the past two years I recieved 2021 University Honors and am a James B. Angell Scholar.

Now that I've told you a little about myself, feel free to reach out and contact me with any questions and take a look at some of my past work!
